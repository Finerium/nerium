## Opus procedural originals

### Opus 4.7 generated canvas atlases

The three world sprite atlases shipped in the RV primary path are procedurally rendered at 32x32 per tile via `scripts/build-atlases-32.mjs`. Frame indices, palettes, and drawing primitives were authored by Opus 4.7 during Talos Sub-Phase 2. Output lives at `public/assets/worlds/{medieval_desert,cyberpunk_shanghai,steampunk_victorian}/atlas_32.png` with matching atlas JSON at `public/assets/packs/<world>.atlas.json`.

The legacy P0 Thalia P3b 16x16 atlases remain in place at `public/assets/worlds/<world>/atlas.png` as fallback and are governed by the repository MIT LICENSE per the original `public/assets/attributions.md`.

### Opus 4.7 generated SVG chrome seeds

Six SVG chrome seeds live at `public/assets/procedural/svg/`: three genre HUD frames (Medieval, Cyberpunk, Steampunk), a genre-neutral minimap ring, a genre-neutral inventory slot, and a NERIUM wordmark logo seed. Hesperus is expected to author polished variants during Sub-Phase 3 and emit supersede entries in the asset ledger. Phaser 3 consumes these SVG assets natively via `this.load.svg()`.
